Cliff Benderoff CPA Inc.

What We Do

We are a firm of chartered professional accountants located in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. We offer a full range of accounting and taxation services. We serve a broad-based clientele, primarily small to medium-sized owner-managed businesses representing a wide spectrum of industries, as well as some large multinational businesses. In particular, we specialize in cross-border taxation matters for Canadians working or doing business abroad and for non-residents working or doing business in Canada.

The firm was founded in 2019 by Clifford Benderoff, CPA, who has over 40 years of experience in public practice. He brings his expertise and experience in Canadian and international taxation.

Our boutique firm offers personalized services in English and French to our clients. We are proud of our commitment to excellence.

We are a member firm of the “Nexia International” network. Nexia International is a leading worldwide network of independent accounting and consulting firms, providing a comprehensive portfolio of audit, accountancy, tax and advisory services.
